How to play Colony

You run an ant colony competing with two rival colonies for the same ground and food. Each cycle (turn) you get 3 Action Points. Spend them, then tap Advance Cycle to resolve the turn.

  • Pulsing green tiles are empty ground next to one of your chambers — tap one to dig a Storage Chamber, Fungus Farm, or Nursery.
  • Pulsing blue tiles are resource nodes you've discovered but haven't connected — tap one to lay a pheromone trail from your nest.
  • Trails take a few cycles to deliver resources, and need spare foragers to run (see the Colony panel below the map to reassign Workers ↔ Foragers ↔ Soldiers).
  • Trails crossing a rival's territory turn yellow (contested). An undefended contested trail can leak resources to raiders — but if you garrison it with soldiers, a raid instead triggers a tactical battle you play out directly: defend your forager and fight off the raiders.
  • Fungus Farms turn sugar into fungus — soldiers and scouts eat fungus, workers and foragers eat sugar, so a standing defense costs upkeep.
  • Hitting certain milestones (total resources harvested, battles won, chambers built, sustained long trails) permanently unlocks colony-wide bonuses.
  • No fixed win condition — the goal is to keep your colony growing and your supply lines safe.
