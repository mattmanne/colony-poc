How to play Colony

You run an ant colony competing with a rival colony for the same ground and food. Each cycle (turn) you get 3 Action Points. Spend them, then tap Advance Cycle to resolve the turn.

  • Pulsing green tiles are empty ground next to one of your chambers — tap one to dig a Storage Chamber, Fungus Farm, or Nursery.
  • Pulsing blue tiles are resource nodes you've discovered but haven't connected — tap one to lay a pheromone trail from your nest.
  • Trails take a few cycles to deliver resources, and need spare foragers to run (see the Colony panel below the map to reassign Workers ↔ Foragers).
  • Trails crossing a rival's territory turn yellow (contested) and can leak resources each cycle.
  • Fungus Farms turn sugar into fungus, Nurseries raise your population cap, Storage Chambers raise how much you can hold.
  • No fixed win condition yet — the goal is to keep your colony growing and your supply lines safe.
